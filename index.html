About Me

Like many artists, photographers, designers, and musicians, my passion stems from feelings of creativity. I’ve not always been an aspiring software developer nor did I believe I would be many years ago. I’ve spent years mastering the saxophone and the art of Jazz, been involved with community events as an Eagle Scout, and have programmed and published my own video game.

From a young age I’ve been fascinated with technology; mixing this with a craving to create has heightened my desire to develop software. One of my dreams is to collaborate with hard-working developers to bring about reliable, scalable, and optimized technology that will aid our society for years to come.

Outside of university, I work with solar engineers to approve homes for the installation of solar panels. When all is said and done, you will find me making music, listening to podcasts, spending time with family, or developing games in the Unity software.
