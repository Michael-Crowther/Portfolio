About Me

Born and raised in Portland, Oregon, I've relocated to Utah for new experiences and to pursue a career in Computer Science. Growing up, I was very active in my community, participating in many high school sports teams such as soccer, lacrosse, and marching band. One of my biggest achievements in life was earning the rank of an Eagle Scout. The lessons taught within that community are some I still use today.

Naturally, I'm a musican and creator. Some accolades worth mentioning include the three Oregon State Jazz Saxophonist awards obtained in my latter years of high school. The creativity within musicianship later translated to the development of software and experimentation of game development.

I'm proud to say that after three years of development, testing, and debugging in the Unity software, my first player horror experience is published and available on Steam. More recently, I've been delving into the world of VR. I anticipate that one day I will be involved with upcoming development in XR for I believe it's the future of portable devices.
